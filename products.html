Tabliya is a traditional Filipino chocolate made from pure, ground cacao beans that are formed into small round tablets. It has a rich, natural chocolate flavor and is commonly used to make hot chocolate or champorado. Because it is minimally processed, tabliya keeps most of the cacao’s natural goodness. BENEFITS AND ADVANTAGES: 1.It has antioxidants that help keep the body healthy. 2.It can boost your mood and energy. 3.It may help keep the heart healthy. 4.It is natural and pure, with no artificial flavors or too much sugar. 5.It can be used in many recipes like drinks, champorado, and desserts.
