TABLIYA is a traditional Filipino chocolate made from pure, ground cacao beans that are formed into small round tablets. It has a rich, natural chocolate flavor and is commonly used to make hot chocolate or champorado. Because it is minimally processed, tabliya keeps most of the cacao’s natural goodness. BENEFITS AND ADVANTAGES: 1.It has antioxidants that help keep the body healthy. 2.It can boost your mood and energy. 3.It may help keep the heart healthy. 4.It is natural and pure, with no artificial flavors or too much sugar. 5.It can be used in many recipes like drinks, champorado, and desserts. NUTRITIONAL VALUE: • It has fiber that helps digestion. •It has healthy fats that give energy. •It is rich in minerals like iron and magnesium. •It has a bit of protein. •It is low in sugar if not sweetened.

CHOCOLATE is a sweet food made from cacao beans. People use it to make candy, drinks, cakes, and many desserts. It tastes rich and creamy, and it can be dark, milk, or white. Many people enjoy chocolate because it’s comforting, delicious, and can even make you feel happy. Benefits and Advantages of Chocolate.It can help boost your mood and make you feel happy. It gives you quick energy when you are tired. Dark chocolate has antioxidants that help protect your body. It can help reduce stress for some people. It is delicious and comforting, making it a nice treat.

SIKWATE tabliya is a warm, rich Filipino hot chocolate made from pure cacao tablets. It has a deep, natural chocolate flavor that’s slightly bitter but comforting, perfect for breakfast or merienda. When mixed with hot water or milk, it becomes smooth, aromatic, and full of authentic cacao taste. It’s a traditional drink that brings a cozy, homemade feel—just like the classic tsokolate our grandparents enjoyed. BENEFITS AND ADVANTAGES OF SIKWATE:It is made from pure cacao, so it is more natural. It can help keep your heart healthy. It gives you energy and helps you feel awake. It has antioxidants that protect your body. It can improve your mood and help you feel relaxed. It is less processed, so it’s closer to real chocolate. It can help warm your stomach and make you feel comfortable. NUTRITIONAL VALUE:Calories for energy Healthy fats from cacao. A little protein. Iron for healthy blood. Magnesium for muscles. A bit of fiber for digestion.

CHAMPORADO is a warm Filipino chocolate rice porridge made by cooking sticky rice with cocoa or tablea. It’s thick, sweet, and usually enjoyed for breakfast or merienda. Many people add a little milk on top to make it creamier. It’s comforting, filling, and perfect for rainy days. BENEFITS AND ADVANTAGES: 1. Gives quick energy Champorado is made from rice, which is a good source of carbohydrates. This helps give you energy for school, sports, or daily activities. 2. Helps you feel full longer Sticky rice and cocoa make the dish filling, so it can keep you satisfied for hours. 3. Contains antioxidants from cocoa Tablea or cocoa has natural antioxidants that help protect the body from harmful substances. 4. Can improve mood Chocolate naturally boosts happy hormones, helping you feel more relaxed and comforted. 5. Easy to prepare and affordable Ingredients are simple—rice, cocoa, sugar, and water—making it budget-friendly. 6. Can be made healthier You can adjust sugar, use muscovado, add fruits like banana, or use low-fat milk. NUTRITIONAL VALUE: (Values may vary depending on recipe) Calories: ~250–350 Carbohydrates: High (main source of energy) Protein: Small amount from rice and milk Fat: Low to moderate (depending on milk used) Fiber: Small amount from cocoa Iron & Magnesium: Present in tablea/cocoa Calcium: From the added milk
